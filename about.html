Est. 1952 · Geneva, Switzerland

A Legacy Forged
in Time

RoyalTime was born in the ateliers of Geneva in 1952, when master watchmaker Édouard Valmont resolved to create timepieces that transcended mere function — instruments of art, legacy, and personal identity.

Seven decades later, every watch bearing the RoyalTime name upholds that founding philosophy: uncompromising craftsmanship, precious materials, and design that endures the passage of time itself.

Why Choose RoyalTime

Four pillars that define our pursuit of excellence

⚙️

Swiss Precision

Every movement is assembled and regulated by master watchmakers in our Geneva atelier, achieving chronometric accuracy certified by COSC.

💎

Finest Materials

We source only conflict-free diamonds, certified gold alloys, and premium sapphire crystal — materials worthy of the name they carry.

🌍

Global Heritage

Worn by royalty, heads of state, and visionaries across 80 countries. RoyalTime has adorned the wrists of history-makers for over 70 years.

🛡️

5-Year Warranty

Every RoyalTime purchase includes a comprehensive 5-year international warranty, complimentary servicing, and lifetime authentication certificates.

Our Mission

"Time is the most precious commodity. We exist to mark it beautifully."

Our mission is to be the world's most respected luxury watch maison — not through volume, but through virtue. We believe that true luxury is not flaunted; it is felt in the cold weight of gold against your wrist, heard in the quiet tick of a perfectly calibrated movement, and seen in the knowing glance of those who recognise genuine excellence.
